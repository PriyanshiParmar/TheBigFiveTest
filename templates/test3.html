About Agreeableness trait

Agreeableness is a personality trait that describes a person's ability to put others needs before their own.
Those who are more agreeable are more likely to be empathetic and find pleasure in helping others and working with people who need more help.
Commonly used to describe the level of friendliness, kindness and even politeness a person displays.
