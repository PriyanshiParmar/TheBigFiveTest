The BIG FIVE Test

Fondly known as the Big 5 Personality Traits, or OCEAN, these are incredibly helpful for understanding yourself. When you understand your own personality, you can ask for your needs, connect more easily, and optimize your behavior.

OCEAN Traits

Openness is when someone is curious and open-minded to new experiences and knowledge. Conscientiousness describes how organized and dependable you are. Extroversion describes how you interact with people. Agreeableness is how you feel toward others. Neuroticism is how you deal with emotions.

Big 5 Test for CAREER

This test allows individuals to better understand what work environment is best suited to them and will enable employers to gauge how well a candidate will perform in a specific work setting. Having this crucial data ahead of time can make the hiring process significantly smoother as the wrong candidates for a position can quickly be weeded out.
