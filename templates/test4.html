About Conscientiousness trait

The Conscientiousness trait (efficient/organized vs. easygoing/careless) denotes a tendency to be organized and dependable, to show self-discipline, to aim for achievement, and to prefer planned rather than spontaneous behavior.
People high in this trait tend to be highly organised, self-disciplined. They stick to rules and are able to prioritise effectively.
People low in this trait tend to be disorganised, procrastinater.
