About Openness trait

Openness is how open-minded, imaginative, creative and insightful a person is or can be.
More open minded people tend to prefer variety, seek new experiences and are curious and perceptive to their environment.
Less open minded people tend to avoid change, dislike disruption and focus on a few specific interests.
